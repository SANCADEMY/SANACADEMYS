What is Sanacademy?

Sanacademy is a startup focused on education, specifically in the field of editing for beginners. Our mission is to provide accessible and comprehensive learning resources to empower aspiring editors.

Editing Curriculum

Our curriculum covers the basics of editing, color grading, video transitions, special effects, and much more. Below is a roadmap of what you'll learn:

  • Introduction to Video Editing
  • Color Grading Essentials
  • Advanced Transitions Techniques
  • Adding Special Effects
  • Mastering Final Cuts
